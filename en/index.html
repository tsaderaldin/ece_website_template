Our Mission

Faculty of Engineering Mission

To prepare qualified engineers through education & training and conduct high-class research to sustain and maintain development of the society.

Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering Mission

To provide high quality education to students that enable them to enhance services to the community through their professional, technical, managerial, communication, teamwork, and research competencies in accordance with Islamic teachings.
